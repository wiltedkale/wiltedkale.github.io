We always wanted more stories from the Koprulu (Terran) sector. A small story in a big world can be a lot more interesting than a big story in a big world, and dwarfs a big story in a small world.

We created some short scenes that take place in the StarCraft universe mainly as practice with this "2.5D" perspective of art: it's tons of work but highly rewarding, and is of course the bedrock of real time strategy games and battle arenas.

Infested Terrain

A Terran outpost gets surrounded. Luckily they have infinite ammo but it doesn't look good. The lone medic is low on energy and needs a nap.

Lost Dragoon

Many klicks from home, a dragoon navigates poorly through arid terrain. He makes a left at the fork but gets attacked, so goes right instead.

Pylon Power

An unsubstantiated claim that pylon fields emit dangerous radiation sparks a bloody skirmish between allies.

Fried Ice

A rogue Terran colony betrays the mineral-only mining agreement in favor of rich vespene gas.
